# Workflow

## Stage 1 — Connect

Connect an authorized supplier catalog and verify the supplier relationship.

## Stage 2 — Filter

Reject products without authorization, active inventory, fulfillment, tracking, defined returns, sufficient data, or calculable economics.

## Stage 3 — Rank

BEACON ranks eligible products and identifies the cheapest falsification test.

## Stage 4 — Verify

VERITY verifies supplier authorization, platform permission, compliance, claims, costs, and margins.

## Stage 5 — Build

DIRECTOR creates approved offer assets. AXIS creates draft listings and integrations.

## Stage 6 — Deploy

After approval, AXIS publishes, synchronizes inventory, and activates order routing.

## Stage 7 — Fulfill

Orders are validated, transmitted to the supplier, tracked, and reconciled.

## Stage 8 — Allocate

Apply the supplier-first financial waterfall and calculate contribution profit.

## Stage 9 — Learn

BEACON measures actual performance and proposes the next product cycle.
